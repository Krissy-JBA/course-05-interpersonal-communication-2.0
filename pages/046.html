What they hear, what you hear

Communication is much more than just talking and listening, and as we saw earlier there's always two perspectives – the sender's and the recipient's.

But don't fall into the trap of thinking that all the hard communicating work is done by the sender!

Yes, they're the ones trying to get their points across, but it turns out that being a recipient requires effort too – at least it does if you really want to understand the info that's being sent your way.
