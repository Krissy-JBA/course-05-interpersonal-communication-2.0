General audience demographics:

  • Size of group
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Education
  • Work background
  • Cultural background

What do they know?

  • Are they uninformed, or well informed?
  • Or is their knowledge level unknown?
  • Would your content be considered 'general knowledge'?

Why are they here?

  • Do they have to hear your message or are they hearing it voluntarily?
  • Do they think they'll get something out of this experience?

What's their attitude likely to be?

  • Think about their attitude toward the content and toward you.
  • Will they be neutral, friendly, hostile, not interested?

What's the best way to reach them?

  • What's in it for them?
  • What info or delivery method will grab their attention?
  • On the flip side, what info or delivery method might get a negative reaction?

What do you want them to do?

  • Make sure you've got a clear action for them to take away.
  • And think about what you want to take away from this experience too!

Will they resist?

  • What things could stop them from taking on your message?
