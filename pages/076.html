Good to know

Most western cultures view eye contact as a sign of trust or confidence.

But, for many Hispanic, Asian, Middle Eastern, and Pacific cultures, eye contact can be considered disrespectful or rude.

If someone won't look you in the eye, don't assume they're trying to hide something - they might actually be trying to show you some respect!
