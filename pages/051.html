Stress

Ever been caught up in a stressful situation when suddenly you're faced with someone asking for help? It probably took all your energy not to tell them to leave you alone, right?! You certainly weren't in any position to actually listen to them!

Stressful situations really aren't good for real listening. So, ask the person if you can get back to them later once you've dealt with the issue in hand - and can help them properly.

Distraction

Most of us have to juggle different functions or roles every day. So you probably know how difficult it can be to take in what someone's saying when you're distracted. That's ok, it happens to the best of us!

Just be up front, apologise nicely, refocus and turn your listening back on!
