Audience

When you're communicating it's really important to have a good think about your audience. Who are they? What are their needs and how can you best address them? How can the information you've got make their lives better? What do you want them to do once the session is over?

These are the sorts of questions you need if you want the content of your communication to be relevant and engaging.

Let's take a look at some great ways to get to know your audience better.
