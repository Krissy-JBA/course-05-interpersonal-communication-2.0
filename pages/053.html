Active listening technique

Stay focussed – pay attention, don't make judgements and be patient.

Really listen – don't work out your response or think about similar experiences, that's all just a distraction!

Allow periods of silence – the other person may need a moment to gather their thoughts.

Repeat back – from time to time, reflect back what you've been told.

Understand their emotions – try and express feelings back to them too.
