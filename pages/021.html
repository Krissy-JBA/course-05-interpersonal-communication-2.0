How to be a good sender

So you've seen how in any face-to-face communication, two people will be involved: the sender and the recipient.

A communication generally starts with the sender, who comes up with a thought and then chooses which words to use to get that thought across to the recipient.

This section of the course is all about being a good sender, and making sure your message makes it to the recipient safely, no matter what the audience or idea are like!
