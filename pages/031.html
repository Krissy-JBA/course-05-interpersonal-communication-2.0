When you're communicating with Owls, be really detailed and well-organised. Stick to the facts and, if you want to sway them, avoid talking about feelings or instincts (that's just not their thing!).

The Eagle

Bold and authoritative, Eagle people can sometimes get a bad rap for being dominant and decisive, but really they're just assertive, decision making types who don't want to mess about!

When you're dealing with an Eagle, make sure you use facts, not opinions; and, if possible, show that you're supportive of their goals and objectives. Eagles will really respect you for dealing with issues in a business-like manner.

The Dove

Dove people are all about diplomacy and tact. Overall, they're people-oriented, sympathetic, supportive team players. They've got good listening skills, are pleasant to work with and you can totally rely on them.

Be warm and sympathetic when you're dealing with Doves. They're very focussed on people, so they place huge importance on feelings and they want the people around them to be happy too.

Doves are naturally relaxed and slow-paced, and are likely to avoid confrontation if there's any conflict.

The Peacock

Peacock people radiate happiness and optimism. They love to talk, and have plenty of enthusiasm for the social aspects of work. Peacocks also love getting recognition for their efforts!

Peacocks aren't interested in details, and they can be impatient (sometimes even a bit manipulative!). When you're dealing with Peacocks, try to avoid long winded arguments; instead, win them over by focussing on showing them that you really support their ideas and visions.
